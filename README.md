# AI Shiksha - AI Literacy Training Platform

AI Shiksha is Bangladesh's first unified AI literacy training platform designed for students, teachers, professionals, and small businesses.

## 🌟 Features

### For Students
- Subject-specific Q&A
- MCQ practice with instant feedback
- Chapter simplification
- Personalized study plans
- Progress monitoring

### For Teachers
- AI-powered lesson planning
- Assessment generation
- Automated student feedback
- Classroom administration tools

### For Professionals
- Role-specific AI training
- Healthcare, Finance, Marketing, Legal tracks
- Skill assessment
- Weekly progress nudges

### For Small Businesses
- Customer service automation
- AI-assisted marketing
- Inventory management
- Business decision support
